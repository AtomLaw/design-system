--- notes: | When using font families, all font weights are at `normal` or `400`. The font families themselves will take care of the visual weight. Some styles change sizes depending on the breakpoint. The Design System's breakpoints are based on Bootstrap's default breakpoints except for the `small` size at `360px`. All breakpoints are using the mobile first approach where mobile is the default. ---

Spacing

All heading elements—e.g., <h1>—and <p> are reset to have their margin-top removed. Headings have a margin-bottom equal to half their line-height. Paragraphs have a margin-bottom equal to their line-height.
