--- notes: | Elevations are applied using the `elevation-` class and are numbered one to five. `.elevation-one` would be the lowest elevation versus `.elevation-five` is the highest. Buttons in most cases start with `.elevation-one` and raise to `.elevation-three` on `:hover`, `:focus` and `:active` states. The `.btn-flat` style of buttons start with no elevation and raise to `.elevation-two` on `:hover`, `:focus` and `:active` states. ---
